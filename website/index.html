AETHERIS

A world that remembers what you did to it

A first-person survival game where the world is alive — and humanity is the infection. Explore overworld ruins, descend into hostile caves, ascend to the Aether, and uncover a truth the game never states directly. Your class is defined by what you carry. Death is punishing. The ending is quiet.

SCROLL

THE WORLD

No Classes — Only Choices

Eight archetypes defined entirely by what you equip. Swap weapons and totems to change your role mid-session. No locked progression trees.

The Corruption

The world has an immune system. Mining, forging, building — cross the threshold and the antibodies come. Undead, headcrawlers, sanity drain. It's not evil. It's biological.

Three Dimensions

Overworld, Caves, and the Aether — a drifting sky realm of floating platforms. Each with unique resources, bosses, and environmental hazards.

Weapon Forging

Smelt ores into liquid, pour into part casts, assemble. Every part contributes stats and traits. Mix within ore groups for synergy bonuses.

Death Has Weight

Die and your corpse becomes an Undead Knight carrying all your gear. Find the beacon, slay your past self — or lose everything to another player.

The Truth

The lore is never stated. It's reconstructed from item descriptions, boss arenas, and notes left by a civilization that built magnificently and died without knowing why.
